How to embed in Notion

1
Convert your files and download the HTML
2
Go to tiiny.host — drag & drop the file and get a public URL instantly (free, no account needed)
3
In Notion, type /embed, paste the URL, and click Embed link
4
Resize the embed block to fit your page — done!
